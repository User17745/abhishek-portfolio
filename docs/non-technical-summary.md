# Non-Technical Project Summary

## Business Objective
Create a professional portfolio website for Abhishek Aggarwal that showcases his 10+ years of multifaceted experience in eCommerce, Product Management, Pre-Sales Solutioning, and Program Management. The portfolio will serve as a comprehensive digital presence to attract potential employers.

## Problem Statement
Abhishek has a rich, diverse career spanning entrepreneurship (TECHNOTRONIC, RigAssembler) and enterprise leadership (GreenHonchos), but currently lacks a centralized digital presence that:
- Consolidates his multi-domain expertise
- Showcases his work with Fortune-500 and global brands
- Highlights his achievements with concrete metrics
- Provides an easy way for recruiters/hiring managers to understand his value proposition

## Target Users
1. **Primary:** Recruiters and hiring managers at tech/eCommerce companies
2. **Secondary:** Potential clients seeking eCommerce consulting
3. **Tertiary:** Industry peers for networking

## Success Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Page Load Time | < 2 seconds |
| Lighthouse Score | > 90 (all categories) |
| Mobile Responsiveness | 100% |
| Contact Form Submissions | Trackable |
| Time on Page | > 2 minutes average |

## Key Features
1. **Hero Section** - Strong first impression with tagline and CTA
2. **About/Bio** - Career narrative with skills overview
3. **Experience Timeline** - Interactive journey visualization
4. **Projects/Case Studies** - Featured implementations with metrics
5. **Brands Section** - Logo showcase of companies worked with
6. **Contact Form** - Direct communication channel
7. **AI Career Assistant (Cookie)** - Interactive RAG-based chatbot for resume analysis and fitment evaluation
8. **Automated Testing Suite** - Ensuring reliable performance across all devices

## Tone & Branding
- Professional yet approachable
- Modern, clean design
- Emphasis on credibility through metrics and brand associations
- Dark/light mode support
